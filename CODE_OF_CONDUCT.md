# Code of Conduct

Netscope contributors and users are expected to keep discussions professional, constructive, and focused on defensive security.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent while asking for evidence.
- Keep safety and authorization boundaries clear.
- Help new users understand responsible scanning practices.
- Avoid sharing sensitive third-party data in issues or discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Requests for exploit delivery, evasion, credential attacks, or unauthorized access.
- Publishing private vulnerability details before maintainers have had a reasonable chance to respond.
- Posting secrets, private targets, or customer data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its users.
